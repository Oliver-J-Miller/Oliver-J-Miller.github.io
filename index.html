Game Jam 2022

This project came from the 2022 Game Jam hosted in Novemeber - a 72 hour event in which small teams create projects with a given theme. this theme was Centenary and My team decided to make a game called CentenaBee: a game in which every 100 years you reincarnate as a bee keeper to help save the bees from the evil Been't organisation. I worked on the main mechanics and making everything work with each other for smooth gameplay

Marble Game

This was my first Unity game made during my time in University. Designing a game in which you launch marbles into an arena to collide with other marbles to obtain them. I am quite proud of how the mechanics came out for my first attempt at scripting in Unity and though the visuals and sounds are not the most interesting aspects I would encourage you to look past and focus on the mechanics and gameplay of the project.

Console Battleship

This is my version of a console made Battleship game, made proceedually as that was the given parameter for the project. We were also limited to very basic programming elements to make it fair on people with less programming knowledge, I believe with this parameters and handicaps I have produce a solid and entertaining game. Though not visually striking(Again an area I dont fully approach) the mechanics and gameplay of the project work incredibly well and scored my highest grade for all of my first year.
